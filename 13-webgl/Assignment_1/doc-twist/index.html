Assignment_1/twist.js

Summary.

Draw a fractal known as Sierpińsk Gasket (with an optional twist factor) by recursively dividing in half the edges of an initial triangle.

The number of red triangles is 3n, and the number of white triangles is the sum of a geometric progression of ratio 3 and first term 1:
  • W(0) = 0
  • W(n) = 3 * W(n-1) + 1
  • W(n) = 1 + 3 + 3² + ... + 3n-1 = (3n-1)/2, n ≥ 0.
