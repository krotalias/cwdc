Keyboard controls (drag on the object to rotate it):
  • b, B - body rotation
  • t, T - torso rotation
  • s, S - shoulder rotation
  • a, A - arm rotation
  • h, H - hand rotation
  • l, L - head rotation
  • o - reset rotation
  • ↑,↓ - Zoom out/in
  • space - toggle intrinsic auto rotate
  • x, y, z - rotation axis
