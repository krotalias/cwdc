keydown

Key handler.

Calls handleKeyPress when pressing assigned keys:
  • Space - pause
  • h - help
  • w, s, a, d - forward, backward, left, right
  • I, K, J, L - orbit down, up, left, right
  • +, - - field of view (zoom)
  • ↑, ↓- up, down
  • n - move camera close/farther away wile rotating, or not
