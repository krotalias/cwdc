Keyboard controls:
  • SPACE - pause rotation
  • a - toggle axes visible/invisible
  • k - toggle texture on/off
  • l - toggle lines visible/invisible
  • x,y,z - intrinsic rotation about a local axis
  • c,C - cylinder/cone
  • d - dodecahedron
  • e - extrinsic/intrinsic
  • i - icosahedron
  • o - octahedron
  • w - tetrahedron
  • p - teapot
  • r - ring
  • v - cube
  • s - sphere
  • t,T - torus/torus knot
  • u - capsule
  • O - Reset to initial state
  • ↑,↓ - Zoom out/in
