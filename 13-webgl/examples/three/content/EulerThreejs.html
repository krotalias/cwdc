Twin Ion Engine (TIE) fighter - Red Baron (Pilot)

Current transformation matrix based on Euler angle convention Yaw-Pitch-Roll.
This requires setting the rotation.order property of the object to "YXZ".

Keyboard controls:
  • h - yaw +15 degrees
  • Y - yaw -15 degrees
  • p - pitch +15 degrees
  • X - pitch -15 degrees
  • r - roll +15 degrees
  • Z - roll -15 degrees
  • l - switch lighting scheme
  • a - toggle axes visibility
  • ↑,↓ - Zoom out/in
  • o - Reset to identity
  • SPACE - toggle auto rotate
