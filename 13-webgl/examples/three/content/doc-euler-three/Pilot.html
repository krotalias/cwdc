Class: Pilot

Pilot()

Airplane Pilot.

Constructor

new Pilot()

Instanciates a new Pilot.

A pilot is made up of of a boby, hair, face, ear (right and left), and a glass. It is a cool pilot with windblown. To simulate fluttering hair, we use only a few boxes.

hairsTop :external:THREE.Object3D

Pilot's hair container.

Hair is made up of a back, side (right and left), and top.
This top portion can be animated. It is a set of 12 boxes (4x4x4),
disposed on a 4 x 3 grid on plane x-z: 
 (-4, -4) (-4, 0) (-4, 4)
 (0, -4)  (0, 0)  (0, 4)
 (4, -4)  (4, 0)  (4, 4)
 (8, -4)  (8, 0)  (8, 4)
