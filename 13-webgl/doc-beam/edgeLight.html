Class: edgeLight

edgeLight(aspect)

Edge light object.

Constructor

new edgeLight(aspect)

Constructs a new edge:
  • initial random position: [-1,1]x{-1,1]},
  • initial random angle: [0,2π),
  • random direction: (cos(angle ± π/2), sin(angle ± π/2))
  • initial size: 0.1
  • random maximum size: [0.5, 1.0],
  • random color from d3 palette,
  • increment: 0.001 (for growth and rotation).
