Keyboard controls:
  • c/C - cylinder/cone
  • d - dodecahedron
  • i - icosahedron
  • o - octahedron
  • w - tetrahedron
  • p - teapot
  • r - ring
  • s,S - threejs/sphere
  • t,T - torus/Torus Knot Geometry (threejs)
  • u - capsule (threejs)
  • v - cube
  • a - toggle axes visible/invisible
  • b - toggle back face culling on/off
  • f - toggle fixuv on/off (sphere by subdivision)
  • k - toggle texture on/off
  • l - toggle lines visible/invisible
  • n,N - next/previous texture
  • m,M - increase/decrease number of subdivisions
  • O - reset rotation
  • x,y,z - extrinsic rotation about a global axis
  • SPACE - toggle arcball/rotation
  • ↑,↓ - Zoom out/in
