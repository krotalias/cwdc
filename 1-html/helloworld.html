The HTML iframe tag specifies an inline frame.

An inline frame is used to embed another document within the current HTML document.

Youtube ___ share ___ embed (copyright issues, sometimes)

YouTube provides an "Embed" option under the share buttons of its videos.

By offering this option, YouTube effectively gives a green light to users to embed videos on their websites or platforms, as long as the original uploader has allowed embedding.

However, ensure the content in the video itself isn't violating copyright rules.
